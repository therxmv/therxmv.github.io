Telegram Themer


This app allows you to fully customize themes with your favorite colors and styles.
- Choose between two distinct styles and personalize further with light or dark mode options
- AMOLED variant for deeper blacks and improved battery life
- You can also enable gradient effects to add depth and dimension to your theme
- For devices running Android 12 and above, take advantage of the Monet accent feature for dynamic color adjustments based on your wallpaper.

Once you've created your perfect theme, easily share it with others via Telegram for seamless setup and use. Available in Play Market.

eRSHU


This application was created specifically for students of Rivne State Humanitarian University (RSHU) to ensure maximum convenience and efficiency in organizing the educational process.

Main functions of the eRSHU application:
- Selecting and saving your course and specialty
- Up to date schedule for the whole week
- Reminders about upcoming lessons

Available in Play Market.
