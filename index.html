projects.

Formula Timer


App provides the exact time remaining to the race start and other information. After install, you can easily access:
- Exact times for the upcoming Grand Prix;
- Schedule for the whole season, including all practices, qualifying, sprints and races;
- Current standings of drivers and constructors;
- Reminder before the start of the race.
Available in Play Market.

Telegram Themer


This app provides for your opportunity to create themes with your favorite color and style. You can choose between two styles. Also, you can select dark or light mode for them, AMOLED variant available too. There is also an option to enable gradient. For Android 12+ available an option to choose monet accent. After setting up style, you can create and share theme file in Telegram, for easy use. Available in Play Market.

Octal Math


This calculator can convert some popular numeral systems. Also, it can calculate arithmetic and geometric progressions. Channel with updates.
